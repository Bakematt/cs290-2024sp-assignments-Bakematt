Academic Persuits:

I am currently pursuing a Post-baccalaureate degree in Computer Science, which stems from my introduction to programming during my tenure at OSU for Manufacturing Engineering. Discovering the significance of this skill in my roles, I found genuine enjoyment in utilizing it. At Sigma Design, for instance, I undertook the design and implementation of databases for tasks such as serial number tracking and other essential functions within the manufacturing department.

Following a layoff at the outset of 2023, I made the decision to pursue a degree in Computer Science due to my deep-seated passion for the field. What particularly draws me to this discipline is the opportunity it provides for designing solutions and engaging in critical problem-solving. Through my coursework at Chemeketa, I have completed numerous CS classes, each strengthening my affinity for computer science.

In my pursuit of further education, I have explored various options and engaged with advisors, ultimately deciding on the Post-baccalaureate program at OSU, drawn in part by my prior experience at the institution.
