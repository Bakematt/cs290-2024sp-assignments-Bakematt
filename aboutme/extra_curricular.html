Tinkering/Electronics

I thoroughly enjoy integrating my passion for woodworking with my fascination for electronics. Recently, I embarked on a project that beautifully melded these interests: crafting Light Signs as gifts for my family and friends during the Christmas season. These Light Signs have quickly become highly coveted presents, appreciated for their unique blend of craftsmanship, creative designs.

This endeavor provides me with the opportunity to engage and challenge myself across various domains that I deeply enjoy. From delving into 3D CAD modeling to mastering CNC machining techniques, from designing intricate electrical circuits to coding, each aspect of the project demands different skills, allowing me to stretch my abilities and expand my knowledge base.

Moreover, this venture also pushes me out of my comfort zone by encouraging me to explore the realm of artistry, an area where I may not always feel entirely confident. Yet, through the process, I've found immense satisfaction in creating visually captivating pieces that combine aesthetics with functionality.
